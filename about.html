The Corner was created in 2019 by Ethan Wilk, a teenager from Arizona (@BitcoinRooster on Twitter). A young developer with backgrounds in video game and iOS development, Ethan fell in love with the technology that powered cryptocurrency and the blockchain. Fueled by curiosity, Ethan began to investigate the white paper behind every altcoin he came across, and eventually took an interest in trading the internet coins in 2017. With a newly found passion for cryptocurrency and for programming, Ethan took it upon himself to create algorithms to trade these mysterious cryptocurrencies. As the operations soon became too messy, he decided to create this very blog you're reading in order to track his progress as a cryptocurrency developer.

This blog is a bit of a mess. If you're reading this right now you're probably only here because Rooster referred you to it or because you have no idea where you are. Either way, on here you'll find posts about cryptocurrency trades (not financial advice, just opinions), my trading algorithms, and my progress at summer camps or internships. Occasionally a guest writer may appear on the blog, maybe to write a post on baking cakes or something I don't really know. If you'd like to stay updated with the madness, please subscribe to our newsletter mail list (can be done at the bottom of any page). Have fun reading!
